The Program

A supportive space to grow.

Make Art Make Money is a 4-week workshop to help you clarify your values, build a supportive network, and grow a creative business that feels true to your work.

1

A small, intentional group

Limited to 10 participants, so the space can stay focused, supportive, and genuinely collaborative. We establish shared community agreements and clear expectations around care, respect, and participation.

2

An exploratory + practical curriculum

Each session blends creative prompts, reflection, and storytelling with concrete tools—helping you move from ideas into structure, language, and real-world application.

3

Committed mentorship + real-world perspective

This course is designed and taught by working artists and creative professionals who are deeply invested in building New Mexico’s creative business ecosystem—and in helping you succeed.

You’ll also connect with creative entrepreneurs, mentors, and the broader ecosystem through Creative Con and beyond.

4

Support from a larger creative ecosystem

This workshop is an entry point into a broader effort to support underrepresented and emerging creative businesses in New Mexico—connecting you to the tools, networks, and opportunities needed to grow sustainably.

You’ll be supported, challenged, and have your vision nurtured in a community of like-minded creatives.
Why This Exists

Artists deserve better business support.

Photo by Sabri Sansoy (@sabrisansoy)

Most business programs weren’t built for artists.
You’re expected to figure out pricing, messaging, funding, and sustainability—often alone, and often in ways that don’t feel aligned with your work.

Make Art Make Money is different.
We start with your creative practice. Together, we identify your gifts, strengths, and the values that drive your work. From there, we build outward—helping you clearly articulate your vision and the impact of what you make, so you can translate your practice into a business that feels genuinely aligned.

We give you the tools, resources and support to move forward with clarity—and the confidence to build a creative business that feels true to your vision.

What You’ll Walk Away With

Clear, actionable plan.

We'll help you better articulate the vision for your creative practice, goals and values you can build a business around.

Photo by Candice Johnson (@journeyfreephotography)

You'll be guided through creative, reflective prompts, hands-on exercises to help you unlock:

1

Clarity on your work and direction

A deeper understanding of your strengths, values, and the role your work plays in your community.

The ability to clearly communicate the impact of your work — whether you’re speaking to collaborators, funders, or fans.
